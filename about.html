Grant Howe

Fractional CTO | Independent Board Member | PE Technology Advisor

Grant currently serves as an independent board member at Cinch Ops and provides fractional CTO and advisory services to PE-backed portfolio companies. His focus is on technology strategy, M&A integration, technical due diligence, and exit readiness—helping companies avoid expensive mistakes and accelerate value creation.

Grant's cross-sector experience spans manufacturing, distribution, field service, nonprofit, and enterprise software—giving him the pattern recognition to identify what works across different technical and business contexts. Recent advisory work includes helping CTOs navigate AI adoption strategies and architecture decisions for companies building new AI-native capabilities.
